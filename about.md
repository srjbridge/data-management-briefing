# About This Briefing

This is a weekly briefing on data management best practices, open data, and emerging trends in Canada and internationally.

**Audience:** Program managers overseeing data governance, open data, and geospatial data management.

**Content:** Curated from Canadian news, government releases, international organizations, academic research, and industry publications.

**Format:** Five sections covering What's New, What's Innovative, What's Controversial, What's in the Lab, and What's in Academe.

**Schedule:** Published every Monday at 8:00 AM ET

**Contact:** Have a story or resource to share? [Submit feedback here](https://github.com/srjbridge/data-management-briefing/issues)
